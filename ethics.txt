Q: Why is AI ethics important?
A: To ensure AI systems are fair, transparent, and do not cause harm.

Q: What is algorithmic bias?
A: Bias occurs when AI systems reflect or amplify unfair prejudices from training data.

Q: How can privacy be protected in AI?
A: By implementing data anonymization, secure storage, and user consent policies.

Q: What are transparency and explainability in AI?
A: They refer to making AI decisions understandable to users and stakeholders.
