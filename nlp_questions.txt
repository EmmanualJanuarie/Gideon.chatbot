Q: What is NLP?
A: NLP is a field of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language.

Q: What are some applications of NLP?
A: NLP is used in chatbots, sentiment analysis, language translation, speech recognition, and more.

Q: What are challenges in NLP?
A: Challenges include understanding context, ambiguity, sarcasm, and variations in language.

Q: What is tokenization?
A: Tokenization is the process of breaking text into words, phrases, or other meaningful elements called tokens.

Q: What is named entity recognition (NER)?
A: NER identifies and classifies key information like names, places, and dates in text.
